Overview

Purpose

Our goal is to provide safe, fun and relaxing white rafting experiences with highly trained instructors and the best equipment available in the market.

Audience

.Who are the target customers? They are people who are looking for some fun and relaxing experiences with family and friends. They are also people who have a good financial situation, since the service isn't very cheap. They must also be fisically fit, at least enought for the activity. Probably people between 20-50 years old. .What do they want? - They want to find nice and safe places to do rafting, they also want to feel safe that the company is actually going to deliver the service in full. .What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They Want to know about location, prices and what it is going to take them to do the rafting. They also probably want to talk to a human responsible for the services, who will be able to answer their questions.
