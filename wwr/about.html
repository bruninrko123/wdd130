History

Bruno's Rafting has been in the market for years. We have provided quality service, that is safe and fun for our clients and offered them the best intructors and the best boats, paddles, safety equipment and etc.

The company started yeas ago when Bruno, as 19 year old boy, bought a boat with his first salary from working in a grocery shop. He started rafting with some of his friends, who were more experienced and he eventually started taking different people on rafting journeys and to charge them for that.
